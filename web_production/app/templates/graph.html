AdaGraph

A suite of graphical modeling tools

About

Adagraph translates a body of text into a graph of nodes and edges where a node represents a token or phrase and an edge represents a non-linear combination of semantic relations learned through a feedforward network on top of AdaNLP tags.

AdaGraph is inspired by multi-document summarization methods, like the ones presented in this paper. Through spreading, AdaGraph learns similar and dissimilar segments of the semantic web. We are hopeful that these methods scale from single-document parsing to thousands of documents.
